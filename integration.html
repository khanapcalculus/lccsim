About Numerical Integration

Numerical integration approximates the area under a curve by dividing it into smaller shapes. Different methods use different approaches: Left/Right Riemann Sums use rectangles at endpoints, Midpoint Rule uses rectangles at midpoints, and Trapezoidal Rule uses trapezoids.
